Raaga club

About

The Raaga Club has always been a huge part of the Student Affairs Program. With melodies and tunes rising from every soul, it sure did achieve in turning the gloomy sky into a bright sunshine sky . Not only that but also added the little extra tinge , that rainbow of seven colours which adds a whole lot of beauty to a clear and a bright sky. This means that the club didn't just help the students of Vardhaman to bring out their talent but also broke opened their shells of fear and insecurity. In every aspect, the club had unity and humbleness and best of all ,Team work.
